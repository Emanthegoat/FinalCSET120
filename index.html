Established in the year of 1909, Sugar Soiree started as a simple bakery named "Bread Box" that only made basic white and wheat bread loafs, and sold baking goods. As time passed and the bakery became more popular, The owner started to experiment and incorporate different types of bread into the business to see how well the changes would do. "Bread Box" soon had new additions like Baguettes, Lemon Bread, Croissants, and Naan.
